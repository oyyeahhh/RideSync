How it works

Set it up in under 5 minutes

One parent creates the group. Everyone else joins by tapping a link sent over WhatsApp. After that, the app handles the daily logistics.

Create the group

Name your carpool, set the destination, and add your home address. Done in 30 seconds.

Invite families

Type each parent’s phone number. They get a one-tap WhatsApp link to sign up.

Set the schedule

Add trips one at a time, or generate a full recurring schedule (e.g. every Tues & Thurs at 5pm).

Drive smart

The app picks the next driver, optimizes the pickup route, and sends it to them automatically.
