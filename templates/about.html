One parent starts the group. Everyone else joins from a WhatsApp link. After that the app picks the driver, plans the route, pings every family, and even tells your kids when to put their shoes on. Nobody coordinates anything.

Set it once. Forget it.

One trip or a whole season of recurring ones — rotation assigns the drivers.

Route arrives two hours before. Tap ✓ as each kid hops in — their family gets a ping.
